The Market Data tab gives you local demographics and current mortgage rates for any deal, so you can evaluate market context without leaving Apers.

## What's Included

### Property Demographics

Apers pulls census-tract-level demographics for any US address. Data points include:

| Category        | Examples                                      |
| --------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| **Population**  | Total population, population density           |
| **Income**      | Median household income, income distribution   |
| **Education**   | Education attainment levels                    |
| **Housing**     | Homeownership rate, median home value          |

<Info>
  Demographics are scoped to the census tract surrounding the property address, giving you a precise view of the immediate area.
</Info>

### Mortgage Rates

Apers provides current national mortgage rates:

- **30-year fixed** rate
- **15-year fixed** rate

These rates update automatically so you always have a current reference point when evaluating financing assumptions.

## How to Access Market Data

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a Deal">
    Navigate to **Deals** in the left sidebar and select the deal you want to review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Market Data tab">
    Inside the deal view, click the **Market Data** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the data">
    Demographics and mortgage rates load automatically based on the property address associated with the deal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Make sure your deal has a property address set. Market data relies on the address to pull the correct demographics for the surrounding area.
</Tip>

## Why It Matters

- **Faster underwriting**: See income levels, population trends, and housing stats alongside your deal files.
- **Financing context**: Reference current mortgage rates while building or reviewing financial models.
- **Single workspace**: No need to switch to external sites for basic market research.
