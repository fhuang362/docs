Inside every deal, you'll find four tabs: **Chat Sessions**, **Summary**, **Deal Info**, and **Market Data**.

## Chat Sessions

This tab shows all sub-chats within the deal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Chat Sessions">
    Open the **Chat Sessions** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View all chats">
    See a list of every sub-chat in this deal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click to open">
    Click any sub-chat to go back to where you left off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Summary

This tab generates an AI-powered summary of your entire deal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Summary">
    Open the **Summary** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Generate Summary">
    Click the **Generate Summary** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View the output">
    Apers analyzes everything in the Data Room and creates a structured summary.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**What you get:**
- Structured output of all key information
- One-paragraph summary of the deal

<Info>
  This uses Apers' built-in ingestion engine to read and understand all your deal files automatically.
</Info>

## Deal Info

This tab shows location information for your deal.

**What's included:**
- Map view based on the property address
- Relevant location-based information

## Market Data

This tab provides market information that Apers has access to.

**What's included:**
- Federal Reserve data
- Other relevant market indicators

<Info>
  Market Data helps you understand the broader economic context for your deal without leaving Apers.
</Info>
