Purchasing from the Marketplace is straightforward. Once you find an item you want, you can buy it and start using it right away.

## How to Purchase

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an item">
    From the Marketplace, click the model or skill you want to purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the details">
    Check the description, preview images, and pricing to confirm it fits your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your purchase">
    Click **Purchase** and follow the checkout prompts to complete your transaction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens After Purchase

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Models">
    Purchased models appear in your **Library** immediately. You can import them into any Deal or Chat from there.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Skills">
    Purchased skills become available in your **Library** and can also be used directly in the **chat system**. They are ready to use as soon as the purchase completes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  All purchases are tied to your account. Your team members can see and use purchased items through the shared Library.
</Info>

## Finding Your Purchases

You can always find your purchased items in two places:

- **Library** — All purchased models and skills appear here alongside your other files
- **Marketplace** — Previously purchased items are marked so you can easily tell what you already own
