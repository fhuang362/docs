The Marketplace is where you find ready-to-use models and skills built for real estate professionals. Browse what's available, purchase what you need, and start using them immediately.

## What's in the Marketplace?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Models" icon="cube">
    Pre-built financial models, underwriting templates, and analytical frameworks you can use in your deals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Skills" icon="bolt">
    Specialized AI capabilities that extend what Apers can do in your chats — from custom analysis routines to domain-specific workflows.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Browse

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Marketplace">
    Click **Marketplace** in the left side panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse or search">
    Scroll through available items or use the search bar to find something specific.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View details">
    Click any item to see its description, preview images, and pricing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Models vs. Skills

|               | Models                                      | Skills                                          |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **What**      | Financial templates and analytical frameworks | AI-powered capabilities for chat                |
| **Use in**    | Deals, Library                               | Chats, Deals                                    |
| **Best for**  | Structured analysis and modeling              | Extending AI responses with specialized knowledge |

<Tip>
  Start by browsing the Marketplace to see what's available before building something from scratch. There may already be a model or skill that fits your workflow.
</Tip>
