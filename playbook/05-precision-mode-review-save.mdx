The Review section shows everything you've defined before you save.

## What You'll See

The review page displays:

- **Investment Thesis**: Strategy name, status, asset classes, property types, risk profile, geography, and objectives
- **Criteria**: All must-pass rules and return targets

## Saving Your Playbook

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review everything">
    Check that all details are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Save">
    Click the **Save** button to create your playbook.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Playbook is ready">
    Your playbook is now available to deploy on any deal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing Later

You can always go back and edit your playbook:

1. Go to **Playbooks** in the left panel
2. Click on the playbook you want to edit
3. Make your changes
4. Save again

<Info>
  Changing a playbook's status to **Inactive** keeps it saved but removes it from active use.
</Info>
