Excel Spreadsheet AI lets Apers create, edit, and control Excel spreadsheets for you.

## What Apers Can Do

|                              | Description                                                                 |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Create Spreadsheets**      | Build models from scratch, like proformas or portfolio trackers             |
| **Use Your Documents**       | Turn scanned rent rolls, OMs, LPAs, and other files into working models     |
| **Clean Messy Data**         | Fix disorganized spreadsheetsâ€”even from screenshots                         |
| **Check for Errors**         | Find mistakes in your existing models                                       |
| **Understand Your Models**   | Apers reads and learns the logic of spreadsheets you upload                 |

## How to Start

Just type what you want in the Chat box.

**Examples:**
- "Create a multifamily proforma"
- "Check this model for errors"
- "Recreate this rent roll from my screenshot"

To use existing files, drag and drop them into the **Data Room** on the right side. Apers will read and understand them automatically.

<Info>
  Apers can turn scanned documents into spreadsheets using its built-in document processing.
</Info>
