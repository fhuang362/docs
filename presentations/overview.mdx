Presentations AI lets Apers create, edit, and manage Google Slides presentations for you.

## What Apers Can Do

|                              | Description                                                                 |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Create Presentations**     | Build decks from scratch, like investor presentations or deal summaries     |
| **Add and Edit Slides**      | Insert new slides, update existing content, or reorganize your deck         |
| **Insert Images**            | Add images to slides to support your narrative                              |
| **Format Text**              | Style headings, body text, and layouts to look polished                     |
| **Use Your Documents**       | Turn deal data, memos, and other files into presentation-ready slides       |

## How to Start

Just type what you want in the Chat box.

**Examples:**
- "Create an investor deck for this deal"
- "Build a market overview presentation"
- "Summarize this deal as a slide deck"

To use existing files, drag and drop them into the **Data Room** on the right side. Apers will read and use them to build your presentation.

<Info>
  Apers creates presentations as Google Slides files, stored directly in your Data Room.
</Info>
