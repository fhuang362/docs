Apers can build Google Slides presentations from scratch based on your instructions and uploaded documents.

## Creating a Presentation

Tell Apers what you need, and it will build a complete slide deck for you.

**What you can create:**

- Investor decks
- Deal summaries
- Market overview presentations
- Property marketing packages
- Any custom presentation you describe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your presentation">
    Type what you want in the Chat box. For example: "Create an investor deck for this multifamily acquisition."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload supporting documents (optional)">
    Drag and drop offering memorandums, financials, or other documents into the Data Room. Apers will pull key information into your slides.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let Apers build">
    Apers creates a Google Slides file in your Data Room with formatted slides, text, and layouts based on your instructions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Your Deal Data

If you already have deal information in Apers, you can reference it directly.

**Examples:**
- "Create a presentation summarizing the deals in my pipeline"
- "Build a deck using the financials from the Elm Street deal"
- "Turn this offering memo into a slide deck"

Apers reads your uploaded documents and extracts the relevant data to populate your slides.

<Tip>
  The more context you provide, the better your presentation will be. Upload supporting documents or describe the deal in detail.
</Tip>

## Where Your Presentation Is Saved

Your presentation appears as a Google Slides file in the Data Room. Click it to preview, or open it directly in Google Slides for further editing.
