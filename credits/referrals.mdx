Apers rewards you for spreading the word. When someone you refer subscribes to a paid plan, both of you earn bonus credits.

## How Referrals Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share your referral link">
    Find your unique referral link in the dashboard under your account settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your referral signs up">
    The person you refer creates an Apers account using your link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They subscribe to a paid plan">
    Once your referral subscribes to any paid plan (Basic, Pro, or Enterprise), the reward is triggered.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Both accounts receive bonus credits">
    Bonus credits are added to your balance and to your referral's balance automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Referral Rewards

| Recipient | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| **You** (the referrer) | Bonus credits added to your balance |
| **Your referral** | Bonus credits added to their balance |

<Info>
  Bonus credits from referrals behave like purchased credits. They do not expire at the end of a billing cycle and are consumed after your monthly plan credits are used.
</Info>

## Tips for Referring

- Share your link with colleagues, partners, or anyone in your professional network who works in real estate.
- There is no limit to the number of people you can refer. Every successful referral earns you more credits.
- You can track the status of your referrals in the dashboard.

<Tip>
  Referral credits stack. The more people you refer, the larger your credit buffer becomes on top of your monthly plan allocation.
</Tip>
