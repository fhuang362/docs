Apers offers three subscription tiers designed for different levels of usage. Each plan includes a monthly credit allocation that refreshes every billing cycle.

## Plan Tiers

<Columns>
  <Card title="Basic" icon="user">
    Designed for individual users getting started with AI-assisted real estate analysis. Includes a monthly credit allocation suitable for moderate, everyday use.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pro" icon="rocket">
    Built for active professionals who rely on Apers daily. Includes a significantly larger monthly credit allocation for heavier usage across chat, modeling, and document processing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enterprise" icon="building">
    Tailored for teams and organizations. Offers custom credit allocations, team management features, and dedicated support.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Basic | Pro | Enterprise |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Monthly credits** | Standard allocation | Larger allocation | Custom allocation |
| **AI chat** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **AI Excel Modeling** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Document processing** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Playbooks** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Workflows** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Team management** | -- | -- | Yes |
| **Priority support** | -- | -- | Yes |

<Info>
  All plans include access to every Apers feature. The primary difference between tiers is the monthly credit allocation and team capabilities.
</Info>

## Choosing the Right Plan

- **Basic** is a good starting point if you are evaluating a handful of deals per month and want to explore what Apers can do.
- **Pro** makes sense once Apers becomes a core part of your workflow and you are running models, processing documents, and chatting frequently.
- **Enterprise** is the right choice when multiple team members need access and you want centralized billing and administration.

<Tip>
  You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
</Tip>
