If you run out of credits before your next billing cycle, you can purchase additional credits directly from the dashboard. Purchased credits are added to your balance immediately.

## How to Purchase Credits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dashboard">
    Navigate to your account dashboard and find the credits section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a credit package">
    Choose from the available credit packages. Larger packages offer better value per credit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Confirm your purchase. Payment is processed through your account's payment method on file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Credits are added instantly">
    Your new credits appear in your balance right away and are available for immediate use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Purchased Credits vs. Plan Credits

| | Plan Credits | Purchased Credits |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Source** | Included with your subscription | Bought separately |
| **Refresh** | Reset each billing cycle | Do not refresh |
| **Expiration** | Unused credits do not roll over | Remain until used |

<Info>
  Purchased credits are consumed after your monthly plan credits are used up. This means your purchased credits serve as a buffer on top of your regular allocation.
</Info>

## When to Purchase vs. Upgrade

- **Purchase credits** if you occasionally need a small top-up near the end of a billing cycle.
- **Upgrade your plan** if you consistently run out of credits before your refresh date. See [Plans](/credits/plans) for tier details.

<Warning>
  Credit purchases are non-refundable. Review your remaining balance and upcoming refresh date before purchasing.
</Warning>
